ACME CORPORATION — IT ACCEPTABLE USE & SECURITY POLICY
Document owner: Information Security
Effective date: 1 January 2026
Version: 3.0

1. ACCOUNTS AND PASSWORDS
- Passwords must be at least 14 characters and include a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
- Passwords must be rotated every 180 days.
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory on all company accounts, with no exceptions.

2. NETWORK ACCESS
- A company VPN must be used whenever accessing internal systems from outside the office network.
- Public Wi-Fi may only be used with the VPN active.

3. DATA CLASSIFICATION
Acme classifies data into four levels:
- Public: may be shared freely.
- Internal: for employees only.
- Confidential: restricted to specific teams; encryption required at rest and in transit.
- Restricted: highest sensitivity (e.g. customer PII, financials); access logged and reviewed.

4. REMOVABLE MEDIA
- USB storage devices are prohibited for Confidential and Restricted data.
- Cloud sync of Restricted data to personal accounts is prohibited.

5. INCIDENT REPORTING
- Suspected security incidents must be reported to the security team within 1 hour of discovery.
- Lost or stolen devices must be reported immediately so they can be remotely wiped.

6. PROHIBITED USE
- No cryptocurrency mining on company hardware or networks.
- No installation of unapproved software on company devices.
- No use of company systems for personal commercial activity.

7. ENFORCEMENT
Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination, and where
relevant, may be reported to the appropriate authorities.
