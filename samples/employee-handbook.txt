ACME CORPORATION — EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK (EXTRACT)
Document owner: People Operations
Effective date: 1 January 2026

LEAVE AND TIME OFF
- Annual paid time off (PTO): 25 days per calendar year, accruing monthly.
- Maximum PTO carryover into the next year: 5 days. Unused days above 5 are forfeited.
- Sick leave: 10 paid days per year, no carryover.
- Parental leave: 16 weeks for the primary caregiver, 4 weeks for the secondary caregiver.
- Public holidays follow the calendar of the employee's country of employment.

WORKING HOURS
- The standard working week is 40 hours.
- Overtime must be pre-approved by a manager and is compensated as time off in lieu.

EMPLOYMENT TERMS
- New employees serve a probation period of 6 months.
- Notice period: 1 month for individual contributors; 3 months for managers and above.

EXPENSES
- Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days with a receipt.
- Meals during business travel are reimbursed up to USD 60 per day.
- Personal entertainment and alcohol are not reimbursable.

CODE OF CONDUCT
- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect.
- Conflicts of interest must be declared to your manager in writing.
- Confidential company information must not be shared outside Acme without authorisation.

PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
- Formal performance reviews occur twice per year, in June and December.
- Promotions are decided in the December cycle.
