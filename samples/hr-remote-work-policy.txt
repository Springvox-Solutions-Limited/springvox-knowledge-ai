ACME CORPORATION — REMOTE WORK POLICY
Document owner: People Operations
Effective date: 1 January 2026
Version: 2.1

1. PURPOSE
This policy defines who may work remotely at Acme Corporation, how often, and the
conditions that apply. It exists to balance flexibility with collaboration and security.

2. ELIGIBILITY
Employees become eligible for remote work after completing 90 days of continuous employment.
Contractors and employees on a performance improvement plan are not eligible.

3. REMOTE WORK ALLOWANCE
Eligible employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with prior manager approval.
Approval is granted per quarter and may be revoked if collaboration or output is affected.

4. CORE HOURS
Regardless of location, all employees must be reachable and online during core hours of
10:00 to 15:00 in their assigned office time zone.

5. EQUIPMENT AND STIPEND
The company provides a laptop to all remote-eligible employees. After 90 days, employees
may claim a one-time home-office stipend of USD 500. An external monitor is reimbursable up
to USD 250 with a valid receipt. Chairs and desks are not reimbursed.

6. LOCATION AND TRAVEL
Remote work must be performed from the employee's country of employment. Working from another
country for more than 20 consecutive days requires written approval from HR and may have tax
implications that the employee is responsible for declaring.

7. SECURITY
Remote employees must follow the IT Acceptable Use Policy, including mandatory VPN use when
accessing internal systems and multi-factor authentication on all company accounts.

8. EXCEPTIONS
Exceptions to this policy require approval from both the employee's department head and the
Head of People Operations. Exceptions are reviewed every six months.
